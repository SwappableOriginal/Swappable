How It Works

Swappable is a domain that will open up a new world of possibilities for every user.

Bringing back "archaic" methods of economic activity, our intention is to aid a huge problem faced by consumers today.

Dating back to 6000BC, the barter system was an ancient method of exchange where goods and services were directly traded without the use of money.

In societies where it was in full practice, individuals or groups would negotiate and agree on the value of their respective items.

For example, a farmer might trade surplus crops for tools made by a blacksmith.

This system relied heavily on mutual need and the double coincidence of wants; both parties had to desire what the other offered.

While effective in small communities, barter became increasingly impractical as societies grew, leading to the development of currency to facilitate trade. BUT! We are bringing it back to not reverse evolution but to help the "I have no money for it" claim that became a norm post-COVID.
